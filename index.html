Lyceum of the Philippines University (LPU), is an institution of higher learning,.

LPU Arc Library

The library is a one floor area located at the third floor of the Mabini Wing. It can seat more than 700 readers at a time.

The library was named after the late Senator Sotero H. Laurel on September 27, 1989 in abiding appreciation to his passion for scholarship, spirit of enlightened nationalism, unceasing quest for the truth and in grateful recognition of his life time dedication to the Lyceum of the Philippines University Senator Sotero H. Laurel is the 2nd LPU President, the 4th child of Dr. Jose P. Laurel, Lyceum’s founding president, and the father of Lyceum’s 3rd President, Atty. Roberto P. Laurel.
